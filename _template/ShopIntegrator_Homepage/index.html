Features

Shop-Integrator is the platform to manage your e-Commerce product data in a centralized way. We connect your shop to your own and your supplier’s or customer’s ERP, CRM, Logistics, warehouse management and wherever else you need data from. Once connected to Shop-Integrator, all updates are synchronized throughout all online-shops, your own ones and your customer’s.

The features below are the out-of-the-box functionalities of Shop-Integrator. Get in touch with us, in case you’re missing any functionality.

Basic Features

  • Management of any e-Commerce system’s product data:
    • Product Details
    • SEO- and Meta-Data
    • Product Variants
    • Categorization
    • Images
    • Availability (Amount On Stock)
    • Delivery Times and Costs
    • Different VAT-taxes by country for B2C online-shops
  • Management of sub-shops / storeviews of your e-Commerce system.
  • Define price groups with your own mark-up prices (either fixed, by recommended retailer price or by percentage of purchase price).
  • Define global price groups for B2C and B2B customers.
  • Define global price groups for different suppliers.
  • Seamless synchronization of article portfolio with e-Commerce.

Advanced Features

  • Multi-Language Support (all languages upon request).
  • Automatic Translation Engine for entire article Portfolio.
  • Management of international e-Commerce systems and platforms.
  • Reuse SKUs in different e-Commerce systems with different data & prices.
  • Define yours and your supplier's shipping costs and, account automatically.
  • Automatic recalculation of prizes based on currency exchange rates:
    • Accounts for exchange rates between your headquarter and the e-Commerce system.
    • Accounts for exchange rates between you and your suppliers for products and shipping.

Custom-Gateway Personalized Product Features

  • Integration with customization engine of "Custom Gateway".
  • Automatic Smart Link, EPA and Custom Gateay related data generation for connecting the e-Commerce to the Custom-Gateway backend.
  • Automatic import and extraction of article data from CG export CSV files.
  • Possibility to change your CPP data, prices, categories and product details

FAQ

Can I re-use my articles for multiple platforms or designs?

  • Yes, our scalable article management system allows for multiple instances of the same product. You can even customize the title, picture or description of an article regarding multiple designs.

Can I change product features or prices after synching?

  • Yes, it is possible to repeatedly synchronize all of your articles with your platforms and designs. Prices can be updated as well as titles, pictures and product descriptions.

Can I add additional platforms on my own?

  • Shop Integrator is a Pay-per-Platform service. That is why platforms will be created by us upon request. However, Shop Integrator allows you to create as many designs as you want does not restrict you in terms of managed articles.
